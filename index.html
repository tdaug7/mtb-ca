GoPro


A GoPro is a small, lightweight camera. The newest edition, called the Hero4 Black Edition,
can record 4k video at 30 FPS. It can also record 240 FPS video at 720p. It can take
12 MP single photos and 12 MP at 30 FPS and a special night photo. It can also do
timelapses in day and night. They let you download "GoPro Studio" which is editing software for GoPro
videos. **WARNING: GOPRO STUDIO ONLY ACCEPTS GOPRO VIDEOS**
